About AKComSocSC '23

All Kerala Communications Society Students Conclave 2023(AKComSoc '23) is a two-day event organized on the 4th and 5th of November 2023. The event is conducted by IEEE ComSoc Kerala Chapter and is hosted by IEEE SJCET SB. Here we are with 2 days of fun, tech, friendship, networking, and much more. Let’s come together and celebrate technology. Come over and discuss the trending changes happening in the world of technology and overnight discussions on different technical domains.

Once in Lifetime Experience

Join us at AKComSoc '23 for a once-in-a-lifetime experience where you can immerse yourself in two days of tech, fun, networking, and insightful discussions on the ever-evolving world of technology.
